### Focusing On Growth
Right now I am focusing on making myself the best version I can be. I am setting aside time everyday to learn, write, create, and reflect. I find myself withdrawing in to minimize and reduce everything back to basics. I am consolidating my efforts and planning out the exact direction that I want to start moving in to acheive my goals for 2023, I have already exceeded them for 2022. This year has been a whirlwind, starting when I unexpectedly lost my day job in January. I made the decision to take my own ventures seriously instead of jumping back into the job market and it is paying off.

I am embarking on a new journey, and releasing into the world a serious of projects that I have been tinkering on for the last year and chronicling my process. I am not entirely sure where the next few months will take me, but I am excited to document my progress.

I first started everyway over 10 years ago as my first business ever. In it's original form it was a basic website design and marketing agency that served my local area. In 2020 I relaunched everyway with a new focus on growth consulting, and I am extremely proud to say that it has been my primary source of income since January 2022. I have been doing a little bit of everythig consulting wise, and I am looking forward to moving into 2023 with a renewed sense of direction and really focusing the business on a specific offering

MDG Growth has since grown and encompass multiple ecommerce projects and currently our biggest project is [Busy Playn][busyplayn] a passion project that has turned into a full blown business ran by myself and my wife
