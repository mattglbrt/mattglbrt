### Focusing On Growth
Right now I am focusing on making myself the best version I can be. I am setting aside time everyday to learn, write, create, and reflect. I find myself withdrawing in to minimize and reduce everything back to basics. I am consolidating my efforts and planning out the exact direction that I want to start moving in to achieve my creative goals for 2023. This year has been a whirlwind, starting when I unexpectedly lost my day job in January. I made the decision to focus on my career and business and secure stability for my family, but now I find myself really missing the creative side and I need to strive to create a balance

I feel like im on the cusp of the next phase of my life, and I want to hone in on what exactly that will be.
