### focusing on the next phase
I feel like im on the cusp of the next phase of my life, and I want to hone in on what exactly that will be. I've spent the last year focusing on building my businesses and consulting career and now I want to come back and focus on my creativity. My goal with this site is to get back to doing the things that I love - writing and painting. I built this site to selfishly have a place to share all of my creations.
