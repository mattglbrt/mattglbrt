I am a husband, father, artist, writer, and consultant. On some days I'm also a gardener, leather worker, designer, developer or just a blob attached to a keyboard and chair. I'm working on fixing that last one.

Professionally, I am the founder of Everyway and MDG Growth. Head of Product Marketing at Invisible Commerce. Growth Consultant for IntellecTechs.

I have spoken at events and taught workshops throughout Virginia on topics such as marketing, website development, small business and startup growth, demand generation, content marketing, and social media marketing.

I currently live in Chesapeake, VA with my wife, daughter, and son. We share our home with two German Shepherds named Pig and Beanifer Jeffries and a feral beast some people might mistake for a cat name, Finnigan.

#### A timeline of my life:

* February 1990 - Born in Waukegan, Illinois.
* 1995 - My parents settle in Virginia Beach after bouncing around a bit due to his father being in the Navy.
* 2005 - I begin working as a paid intern in the IT department at a place called Coastal Training Technologies.
* 2008 - Dupont purchases Coastal. I get my first taste of how miserable working at an enterprise company is. I do not learn from this experience.
* 2008 - I start attending Old Dominion University on a full ride academic scholarship.
* 2009 - After 4 years of bouncing around various IT, Network Admin, Marketing, Design, & Development roles I leave Dupont to go work at Inmotion hosting as a tier 1 advance support rep.
* 2009 - Within 6 months of working Night Shift phone support, I set record numbers in performance, but lose all faith in humanity.
* 2009 - I left Inmotion to join my first start up - Infinite Gaming Abyss. Fails
* 2010 - I start working as a Marketing Associate at IntellecTechs. Over the course of three years I rise to be Head of Marketing handling about 80 clients.
* 2011 - After multiple side hustles and half ass attempts at starting businesses, I start my first real business, Nimbus Hookah Lounge. This also fails.
* 2012 - I graduate ODU with a degree in Psychology.
* 2013 - I leave IntellecTechs to be Marketing Manager at a local tech company. 
* 2014 - I create Everyway Design, my own design and marketing agency. Success.
* 2015 - I was a little too successful for my level of maturity and a way too disrespectful to the IRS.
* 2015 - IntellecTechs reaches out to me and asks me to come back right as I am getting used and abused by the IRS, I accept thinking it will be a temporary fix to my tax problems.
* 2019 - I get married.
* 2020 - My daughter is born.
* 2021 - I leave my full time position at IntellecTechs and join a startup MoovWeb. Perform a successful rebranding, help drive record setting growth and become acquired by Limelight.
* January 2022 - After a string of promotions, I am fired from Limelight after going through the acquisition, I would later be hired back as a consultant.
* February 2022 - I made the decision to start taking my own businesses seriously, and set out to land my first clients.
* March 2022 - My son Lincoln is born.
* August 2022 - After months of grinding, I have achieved stability through my own businesses
* October 2022 - I decide that I want to start blogging this journey in public and possibly start writing a book.
