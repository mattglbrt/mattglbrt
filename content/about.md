Matt Gilbert is a husband, father, artist, and somedays a marketing & growth professional. On other days he's a writer, painter, gardener, leather worker, designer, developer or just a blob attached to a keyboard and chair. He is working on fixing that last one.

Professionally, he is founder of Everyway and MDG Growth. Head of Product Marketing at Invisible Commerce. Growth Consultant for IntellecTechs.

He has spoken at events and taught workshops throughout Virginia on topics such as marketing, website development, small business and startup growth, demand generation, content marketing, and social media marketing.

Matt Gilbert lives in Chesapeake, VA with his wife, Michelle,  daughter, Thea, and son Lincoln. They share their house with two German Shepards named Pig and Beanifer Jeffries and a feral beast some people might mistake for a cat named, Finnigan.
