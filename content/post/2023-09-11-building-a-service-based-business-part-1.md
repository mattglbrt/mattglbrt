This post serves as the Table of Contents and the roadmap for my first series of content on this website, How to Build a Service Based Business. I have done this countless times for clients and customers and now I am doing it for myself. Again. This time I am doing my best to document everything and build it in public.

## Legal Stuff
I obtained a Federal EIN, State Business License, and Resale Tax Cert all online. From there I took those documents to my city municipal center and obtained a local business license because my business will operate out of my house for now. This is also where I will say **This is a retelling of what I personally did. I am not telling you this is what will work for you. I am not a lawyer or financial professional in any way, shape, or form. You are not my client. This is not advice.** The local license cost another $50 that I had to pay in person this time, but going forward I will be able to mail a check each year to keep renewing.

### Obtaining a State Business License
This is going to change based on every state. I also have no idea how it works outside of the US but I have compiled a list of every state's small business portal. 

In Virginia, I was able to go online and obtain an LLC in about 15 minutes for $100 which is the fee in my state. No legal zoom or expensive third party needed. 

I compiled [a list of every state's small business website](https://mattglbrt.com/post/2023-09-11-state-specific-small-business-resources-across-the-u.s) in case anyone wants to take a look at how it works in their state.

### Obtaining a FEIN ( Federal Employement Identification Number)
