## Increase Watch Time
* Do not use intros because you need to retain 50%+ viewer longer than 30 seconds. 
* Immediately start with a hook
* Optimize for suggested, people searching don't have long watch time 
* View duration is becoming the most important metric, even moreso than thumbnail click through
* Create long for videos or shorts, 10m videos not as good anymore
* Youtube is changing shorts to start driving people to your long form
* Use the shorts as hooks for long form
* Do not max out end screens now - just have a subscribe button and then a specific video to watch next
* Don't use chapters because it hurts watch time - this is deliberately user unfriendly though so millage may vary

## Create a Avatar
* Why are you making videos? Who is it for?
* Create a viewer avatar, and make videos for that specific person
* What are the pain points for this one person?
* What do they want to learn
* Where else do they find info
* Where do thy hang out
* What questions do they have

## Market Research
* Research successul people, check their comments for opportunities for content
* Start with at least 4 videos
* Use tube buddy or vidiq for research
* Find titles that are working for other people
* For titles: Front load search term, backload emotional trigger
* vidiq will recommend titles as well

## Script Writing
* Use a powerful statement as a hook
* Write scripts with 5 main takeaways for your audience and follow up recommendation
* Have multiple filming areas that are recurring
* 3-4 hours per video filming at the start
* Film in clips to make editing easier
* https://frame.io for video editing collab
* Upload default settings to update description automatically for each upload
