# United States SBA & State Business Licensing Resources

Starting a business in the United States usually begins at the **state level**, but many entrepreneurs don’t realize that certain cities and counties also require their own **local business licenses, permits, or registrations**. Requirements vary widely depending on your industry and location.  
Before you begin operating, be sure to check:

- Your **state’s business licensing portal**  
- Your **city or county government website** for local permits  
- Any **industry-specific regulations** (food service, childcare, professional licensing, etc.)

The directory below provides direct links to every **SBA district office** and every **state business licensing portal**, giving you a clear starting point for launching your business.

This is focused on the United States simply because I am unfamiliar with licensing in other countries.
