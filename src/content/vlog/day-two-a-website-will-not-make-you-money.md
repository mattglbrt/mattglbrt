## What a Website Is NOT

Let me be clear about this:

**A website is NOT a lead or sales generating tool.**

By itself, a website does not generate leads. It does not generate sales. If you build a website and expect customers to magically appear, you're going to be disappointed.

Too many people think "I'll build a website and the money will come." That's not how it works.
